===============================================
    WORD WIZARD - English Vocabulary Learning App
    For Children Ages 5-7 (UK Key Stage 1)
===============================================

QUICK START (Just 2 steps!)
---------------------------

Step 1: Open the app
   - Find the file called "index.html" in this folder
   - Double-click it to open in your web browser

Step 2: Start learning!
   - Select a word category (Home, School, Animals, etc.)
   - Click "Start Session" to begin the 15-minute timer
   - Play the games and earn stars!

That's it! No installation needed.


HOW TO USE THE APP
------------------

FOR DAILY 15-MINUTE SESSIONS:

1. Open index.html in your browser
2. Click "Start Session" to begin the timer
3. Choose a category (e.g., "Home", "School", "Animals")
4. Play the games:
   - Picture Match: Match words to pictures
   - Spelling Bee: Spell words using letter tiles
   - Story Time: Read interactive stories with highlighted vocabulary
5. Earn stars for correct answers!
6. When the timer ends, see your results


FOR PRINTABLE MATERIALS (Offline Practice):

1. Click "Print Materials" in the navigation
2. Select a category or "All Categories"
3. Choose what to print:
   - FLASHCARDS: Word cards with pictures and definitions
   - ACTIVITY SHEETS: Matching, tracing, and fill-in-the-blank exercises
   - WORD POSTERS: Colourful posters to display on walls
4. Click "Generate PDF" and print!


RECOMMENDED DAILY ROUTINE
-------------------------

Morning (5 mins): Review flashcards from previous day
School Time: Use printed activity sheets
Evening (15 mins): App session with parent supervision
Bedtime: Look at word posters on the wall


WHAT'S INCLUDED
---------------

VOCABULARY CATEGORIES (140 words total):
- Home (kitchen, bathroom, garden, etc.)
- School (classroom, teacher, homework, etc.)
- Family (parents, grandparents, cousin, etc.)
- Food (breakfast, sandwich, vegetables, etc.)
- Clothes (trousers, jumper, wellies, etc.)
- Animals (hedgehog, squirrel, robin, etc.)
- Playground (seesaw, hopscotch, conkers, etc.)
- Weather (sunshine, rainbow, thunder, etc.)
- Body Parts (heart, lungs, skeleton, etc.)
- Colours & Shapes (triangle, hexagon, pattern, etc.)

All words use British English spellings and are aligned
with the UK National Curriculum Key Stage 1.

TECHNICAL REQUIREMENTS
----------------------

Works on any computer with a modern web browser:
- Windows: Chrome, Firefox, Edge
- Mac: Safari, Chrome, Firefox
- No internet required (works offline!)
- No installation needed
- No cloud server needed


PRONUNCIATION FEATURE
---------------------

The app includes text-to-speech pronunciation.
This uses your computer's built-in voice, so:
- On Mac: Uses the UK English voice
- On Windows: Uses the default English voice

Click the speaker button (🔊) to hear any word!


TIPS FOR PARENTS
----------------

1. KEEP SESSIONS SHORT: 15 minutes is ideal for this age
2. CELEBRATE PROGRESS: Focus on stars earned, not mistakes
3. MAKE IT FUN: Let children choose their favourite categories
4. USE PRINTABLES: Physical materials help reinforce learning
5. DISPLAY POSTERS: Visual reminders throughout the day
6. PRACTICE DAILY: Consistency is key to vocabulary growth
7. TALK ABOUT WORDS: Use new words in everyday conversation


TROUBLESHOOTING
---------------

Q: The app won't open?
A: Right-click index.html and choose "Open with" -> your browser

Q: Sound not working?
A: Check your computer's volume. The speaker button uses
   your browser's text-to-speech feature.

Q: Printing doesn't work?
A: Make sure your browser allows pop-ups for this page.
   The print preview opens in a new window.

Q: App looks strange?
A: Try using Chrome, Firefox, or Safari browser.
